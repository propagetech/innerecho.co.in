We are with you!

At Inner Echo, we are a group of passionate and empathetic counsellors in Bangalore, we are here for you – there is no judgement, no prejudice – no matter what is troubling you, we are here to listen to you.

Your state of mind could be due to any reason - depression, trauma, grief, anxiety, stress issues, behavioural issues, sexual abuse, weight issues, domestic violence or psychotic disorders, we will find our way to help you cope, deal or resolve together.

And it’s not necessary that you come to us, because no matter where you are, we will come to you via virtual counselling sessions – our counsellors and therapists are available vie audio and video calls.
