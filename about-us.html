ABOUT US

The entire idea behind setting up InnerEcho was to offer an encouragement, a gentle nudge to those who are living with behavioural and emotional issues to speak out and get the help they need and deserve. As one of the leading counselling centres in Bangalore, we aspire to create a positive impact in the society – this we try to do by not only offering support, but also providing positive intervention for a large range of behavioural and emotional issues.

Today, we can proudly claim that we have hundreds of satisfied counselees. In our aim to do our bit for the society, we provide free consultation for healthcare workers, Covid patients and to families affected by the pandemic. Our free services also extend to acid attack victims, people in palliative care and ones with financial distress. Our aim continues to be one – give people the chance to fight and get through their toughest times in life.

Founder's Note

As a founder of Inner-Echo, I get asked one question very frequently, "What is my vision". That put me into thinking if I have a vision in mind for Inner-Echo, in the process I realized, a vision is not that vital if the journey is contented with constant movement and larger welfare is involved.

The idea is to keep walking, taking small steps towards better mental health and the well being of each one in our sphere as well as the people reaching out to us. The idea is to keep adding people to the caravan and build a community of people who are mindful of others and their overall health and keep touching lives.

Where we try to attain ways of living life as beautifully quoted by Dr. Ali: "Peace within and harmony with others". At Inner-Echo we believe and practice, "Everything YOU believe and feel is Valid."
